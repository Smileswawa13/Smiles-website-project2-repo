WELCOME TO CNHS

We are thrilled to extend a warm and hearty welcome to all students, parents, guardians, teachers, staff, and visitors to our school's digital home. Our website is more than just a virtual space; it's a portal into the heart of our educational community. Here, you will find a wealth of information about our academic programs, extracurricular activities, events, and the dedicated individuals who make our school a hub of learning and growth. At Calapagan National High School, we are committed to nurturing inquisitive minds, fostering creativity, and inspiring a love for lifelong learning. This website is your gateway to explore the myriad opportunities and experiences that await you within our school's vibrant walls. Whether you are a prospective student seeking to join our family or a current member looking for updates and resources, we invite you to navigate through our digital corridors with curiosity and enthusiasm. We encourage you to stay connected, stay informed, and stay inspired as you embark on this educational journey with us. Thank you for being a part of the Calapagan National High School community, where we believe in

"Education with Excellence." Warm regards, Principal Benilito Saromines
