About our School

Calapagan National High School stands as a beacon of education and community in our region. Nestled amidst picturesque surroundings, our school embodies a commitment to academic excellence, character development, and community engagement. With a dedicated faculty, diverse student body, and a wide range of educational opportunities, we aim to empower our students to reach their full potential. At Calapagan National High School, we nurture not only minds but also hearts, fostering a strong sense of responsibility and citizenship. Together, we strive to create a vibrant and inclusive learning environment where every student can thrive and contribute positively to society.

About our Students

The students of Calapagan National High School are a diverse and dynamic group, representing a vibrant tapestry of talents, backgrounds, and aspirations. Nestled within the heart of the Calapagan community, these young individuals bring a sense of unity and purpose to their school. With an unwavering commitment to academic excellence, they engage in a wide array of educational pursuits, from science and mathematics to arts and humanities, all while fostering a culture of inclusivity and collaboration. Beyond the classroom, these students are active participants in various extracurricular activities, showcasing their skills in sports, music, drama, and community service. They embody resilience in the face of challenges and possess a strong sense of responsibility towards the betterment of their society. As the future leaders and change-makers of Calapagan, these students shine as beacons of hope, embodying the spirit of progress and unity that defines their school.

About our Faculty

The faculty of Calapagan National High School is a dedicated and passionate group of educators committed to fostering excellence in education. With their diverse expertise and unwavering dedication, they create a vibrant and supportive learning environment where students can thrive academically and personally. These educators not only impart knowledge but also instill values of integrity, curiosity, and community engagement. Their tireless efforts and innovative teaching methods empower students to reach their full potential, making the faculty an integral part of the school's mission to prepare the next generation of leaders and responsible citizens.
